Playwright & Direction: "मैं लेडिज हूँ, ये जैंट्स हैं" / (I am ladies, he is gents) A nukkad-natak based on a goat-rape that happened in Haryana, India in 2018. "खयाल" / (Ponder) A staged-play that depicts a story of a mid-30s stone-worker, from Chandni Chawk, is captivated by a disease that makes him talk incessantly. "अभी बाकी है" / (It Still Remains) A political satire based on a “mysterious wall” in a room that acts as a king and guides his kingdom.
